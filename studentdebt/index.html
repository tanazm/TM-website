Biden considers forgiving more student debt, faces strong opposition

By Tanaz Meghjani April 29, 2022

President Biden confirmed heâ€™s seriously considering canceling more student debt, as some Democratic lawmakers have been urging him to do, but critics worry about the impact on inflation.

Biden has already forgiven about $16 billion in student debt for approximately 550,000 borrowers who were defrauded by their institutions, have permanent disabilities, or are public service workers with income-driven repayment plans.

But this amount comprises just four percent of what he promised to forgive on the campaign trail and falls significantly short of the $50k per borrower leading Democrats have been urging him to forgive since he took office.
